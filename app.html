A coding exercise that compares numbers (fizz and buzz) to see if they are multiples of numbers in a list. If they are multiples of just one: the output is either Fizz or Buzz. If both numbers are multiples, the output is FizzBuzz.

Instructions

Enter a number for Fizz and a number for Buzz to see if they are multiples of numbers in a list from 1 to 100.
